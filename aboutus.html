ABOUT US



The State Bank of India is an Indian multinational, public sector banking and
financial services statutory body. It is a government corporation statutory body headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. SBI
is ranked as 216th in the Fortune Global 500 list
of the world's biggest corporations of 2018.
